About Us

Jaferia Islamilainen Yhduskunta is a shia islamic center for Pakistani urdu speaking community. It is located in Espoo, Finland. Our goal is to support Finland's growing urdu speaking shia Muslim community and promoting the true message of Islam. Our doors are always open to all Muslims and those interested in Islam.

Programs

We organize regular weekly programs. In addition to this, various events and special programs are organized which are announced via relevant channels. Programs are held mostly in Urdu and English language.


Regular Programs:

  • Tafseer-e-Quran on Tuesdays at 7.30pm
  • Du'a Kumail on Thursdays at 7.30pm
  • Lectures on Saturdays at 7.30pm

Services

We offer various services and weekly programs for urdu speaking islamic community in Finland. We serve mainly in Urdu language but we welcome muslims from all countries.

Events

Ayyam e Fatima

3 days of Ayyam e Fatima were observed on acoount of martydom of Syeda Fatima (AS). During this event, the aspects of the life of the daughter of the Holy Prophet (PBUH) were highlighted.

Muslim Unity Conference

We celebrated the auspacious occasion of Eid e Milad un Nabi by having a muslim unity week according to suggestion of Imam Khomeini (RA). Muslim scholars representing different sects were invited to the event to promote religious harmony and msulim unity.

Arbaen e Hussaini

On the occasion of 40th day from the martydom of Imam Hussain (AS), we observe this tragic day highlighting the sacrifices made by Iman Hussain (AS) and his companions and focus on the lessons and our responsibilites towards our Imam.

Ayyam e Muharram

The followers and lovers of Ahlalbait (AS) gather to commemorate and grieve on the tragic martydom of Imam Hussain (AS). Majalis are held to remember the great sacrifice given by nephew of the Holy Prophet (PBUH) and his companions in the land of Karbala.
